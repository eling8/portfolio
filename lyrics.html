Automatically generating song lyrics based on artist style and theme

An AI model that generates song lyrics in the style of a particular artist, based on a theme or topic.

The goal of this project was to generate song lyrics in the style of a particular artist, based on a theme or topic. There were two major aspects of the project for our task. The first was figuring out how to cluster songs in such a way that cohesive themes would arise. The second was using AI techniques to generate lyrics in the style of the artist.

  • Created a lyrics dataset from azlyrics.com of approximately 120 popular artists, with a total of over 6000 songs.
  • Used K-means clustering on song lyrics to create groups of songs with similar ideas.
  • Used an N-gram Markov model to generate lyrics.
  • Evaluated using a rubric to score actual songs vs. generated songs

For our final model, we decided to use NLTK to filter out words that were not nouns, verbs, adjectives or adverbs. We then used a Snowball stemmer to get the stem of each word. We realized that words like ‘loving’ and ‘love’ should be in similar themes, but they were separate features in our current model. We created a song-word matrix again, but with the filtered, stemmed words. We also used TF-IDF and SVD to collapse the matrix.
